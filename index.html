// About Me
A diligent scientist driven by
curiosity and real-world impact

I am a Kenyan-born climate scientist and geophysicist currently pursuing my PhD at AIMS Research and Innovation Center in collaboration with Imperial College London, developing multi-sensor deep learning frameworks for flood depth estimation in Rwanda's mountainous cities.

My academic journey spans four countries — Kenya, Senegal, Rwanda, and the UK — earning a BSc with First Class Honours, an MSc with Distinction from AIMS-Senegal, and pursuing an MSc in Geophysics at ICTP-EAIFR.

I also serve as IT Manager at ACMHR-K (Alliance for Cardio-Metabolic Health & Research – Kenya), am a member of the AXIOM Research Group at AIMS-RIC, and have built the websites for both organizations.
